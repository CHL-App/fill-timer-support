Privacy Policy · Last updated May 2026

Overview

Fill-Timer ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

Fill-Timer does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to any external server operated by us.

Information Stored Locally

  • Birthday — entered voluntarily in the Life Clock feature. Stored only on your device using iOS/Android system storage. Never transmitted.
  • App preferences — theme selection, timer settings. Stored locally only.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob — used to display banner advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads, subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Users who purchase Premium (ads removed) are not subject to ad data collection.

Apple In-App Purchase — purchase transactions are processed by Apple and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store payment information.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or any identity information
  • Location data
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Browsing history

Push Notifications

Fill-Timer requests notification permission solely to alert you when a timer ends. Notification content is generated locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

Fill-Timer does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. The app contains advertisements (for free users) which are served by Google AdMob with non-personalized settings.
